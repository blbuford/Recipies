# Beef Koftas
## Ingredients
- Ground Beef | 2 lbs
- Onion | 1 medium yellow | Minced
- Parsley | ~ 1/3 to 1/2 cup | Diced
- Garlic | 3 cloves | Minced
- Coriander seed | 2 tsp | roasted and ground
- Cumin seed | 2 tsp | roasted and ground
- Cinnamon | 1/4 tsp 
- Sumac | 1/2 tsp
- Salt | 1 1/2 tsp
- Black Pepper | 10 cranks
